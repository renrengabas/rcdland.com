Property Description

Discover the cool serenity of RCD Royale Homes Silang Phase 4! The Phase 4 of RCD Royale Homes Silang has a total lot area of 23,982 sqm with 152 3 bedroom single-attached units. It is a well-planned community that is complete with amenities such as a guarded entrance gate, basketball court, swimming pool, jogging path, clubhouse, and a pocket garden with outdoor bench. Your kids can also enjoy their very own playground which features larong pinoy activities such as piko and patintero. The subdivision is strategically located in Brgy. Kalubkob, Silang, Cavite which is just minutes away from schools, public markets, hospitals, churches and shopping malls. Getting to and from the Metro is hassle-free with public transportation that is easily accessible few minutes from E. Aguinaldo Highway. The subdivision has direct access to East-West Road and is only a 15-minute short drive from Cavite-Laguna Expressway (CALAX). The cold breeze can be felt with Tagaytay being 10 minutes away from the property. Set within a scenic and relaxing environment, this home is definitely your breath of fresh air from the city. For as low as ₱5.6 million, you can enjoy the serene lifestyle here in RCD Royale Homes Silang Phase 4!

How to Get There

Via Commute

  1. Ride a van to Robinson’s Dasmariñas.
  2. Take a jeep or bus to Silang/Tagaytay, ask to be dropped at Premiere Plaza.
  3. From Premiere Plaza, ride a tricycle to RCD Royale Homes Silang.

Via Private Vehicle

Via SLEX:

From Starmall Alabang, take SLEX, exit at Carmona, go straight. At SM Dasma, turn left toward Tagaytay via Aguinaldo Highway. Nearest landmark is Robinsons Premiere Plaza.

Via Daang Hari:

Drive along Daang Hari Road, pass District Imus Mall, then turn left toward Tagaytay via Aguinaldo Highway.
