Property Description

For that balanced lifestyle, RCD Royale Homes Bulacan is the best choice for you! Flourishing as the next growth area of the North, Bulacan has the perfect blend of urban and rural living with the presence of both business developments and tranquil communities. Various infrastructure and business developments are being initiated in the province of Bulacan. RCD Royale Homes Bulacan is a housing project consisting of 624 units in 7 hectares of land. Modern-designed townhouses and single-attached homes are available for as low as P900,000 to P1.7 million. In close proximity to the metropolis, RCD Royale Homes Bulacan is the perfect home for the city-dwellers who wants a relaxing environment after work.

How to Get There

Via Commute

  1. Bus: There are a lot of bus lines you can choose with when you want to go at RCD Royale Homes Norzagaray, Bulacan if you will be coming to Metro Manila these buses depart from Epifanio De Los Santos Avenue / Congressional Avenue Intersection, Quezon City station. Just simply ride buses with the signage of S.Palay (Sapang Palay) and ask the bus driver to drop you at Road 2 Sapang Palay. From there simply walk going to RCD Royale Homes Norzagaray, Bulacan.

Via Private Vehicle

Via NLEX / Pan-Philippine Hwy / R-8:

When driving your own vehicle going to RCD Royale Homes Norzagaray, Bulacan, take EDSA (Epifanio De Los Santos Avenue) taking the North bound side. This will require you to a lot at least 1 hour and 30 minutes of travel time going to our site. Get on NLEX /Pan-Philippine-Hwy/ R-8 in Bocaue From Norzagaray/ Santa Maria Rd then Santa Maria Bypass Rd. At the roundabout, take the 3rd exit onto Norzagaray- Santa Maria Bulacan and continue until you reach Road 2 Sapang Palay. In there, you will see RRH Norzagaray, Bulacan’s Site.
